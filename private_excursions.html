Our Private Tour Options

Appleton Rum Estate Tour

Discover the history of Jamaican rum. This tour takes you through the distillery process and includes a tasting of some of the finest rums.

YS Falls Tour

Explore the serene and picturesque YS Falls. Swim in the natural pools and relax in the peaceful gardens, away from the crowds.

Black River Safari

Take a guided boat tour down Jamaica's longest river. Spot crocodiles and learn about the local ecosystem and history.

Pelican Bar

Visit this iconic rustic bar built on a sandbar in the middle of the Caribbean Sea. A one-of-a-kind experience.

Mayfield Falls & Sunset at Rick's Cafe

Hike to the "Washing Machine" at Mayfield Falls, then end the day with cliff diving and sunset views at Rick's Cafe.

Negril Tour

Experience the laid-back vibes of Negril. Visit Seven Mile Beach, go to local shops and markets, and watch the sunset at Rick's Cafe.

Bob Marley Yaad Tour

A pilgrimage to the birthplace and final resting place of the "King of Reggae." Immerse yourself in the life and legacy of Bob Marley.

Kingston Tour

Discover Jamaica's vibrant capital city. This tour includes landmarks like the Bob Marley Museum, Devon House, and a taste of the city's unique culture.

Benta Falls

Hike to the beautiful, less-traveled Benta Falls. Enjoy the natural beauty and refreshing waters of this hidden gem.

Doctors Cave Beach & Montego Bay Cultural Tour

Combine relaxation with a cultural exploration of Montego Bay. Enjoy the famous beach before a guided tour of the city's historic highlights.

Croydon Plantation Tour

Learn about coffee and pineapple production on this beautiful working plantation. Enjoy a delicious meal with fresh produce from the farm.

Dunn's River Falls

A classic Jamaican adventure where you can climb the famous terraced waterfalls. This is a must-do for any visitor to the island.

Blue Hole

A secluded and tranquil spot with stunning blue natural pools. Jump in from a rope swing and climb the cascades for an exhilarating experience.

Martha Brae River Rafting

Glide down the serene Martha Brae River on a 30-foot bamboo raft, poled by an expert guide. Learn about the local flora and fauna and enjoy the peaceful journey.

Luminous Lagoon

Witness the magic of the Luminous Lagoon, one of the few places in the world where bioluminescent microorganisms light up the water at night.
